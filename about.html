Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence, inspiring innovation, critical thinking, and lifelong learning in students.

Who We Are

Located in the heart of Wah Cantt, FG Science Degree College is a premier institution affiliated with the National University of Pakistan (NUP). Established in 1993, our college spans over 60 canals of lush green grounds, offering a serene and conducive environment for academic and personal growth.
FG Science Degree College is one of the region’s most prestigious institutions, known for its disciplined environment, experienced faculty, and modern learning resources. Since its establishment, the college has remained committed to nurturing students’ intellectual and personal growth.
