Admission Information

Your gateway to a legacy of excellence

Admission Procedure

  • Admission forms are accepted until the deadline announced by FGEI Directorate.
  • Eligible candidates' lists and interview schedules are displayed on notice boards.
  • Admission is confirmed after the Principal's approval and fee submission.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% marks in SSC required for HSSC admission.
  • Only real sons of Armed Forces, FGEI, POF employees (serving/retired) & open merit for Cantt residents.
  • Applicants from Humanities (SSC level) not eligible for General/Computer Science group.

Admission Policy

Admissions are granted per policy of FGEI (C/G) Directorate GHQ Rawalpindi. The program is announced via POF Khabarnama and regional FGEI office Wah Cantt, typically within a week after FBISE result declaration.

Required Documents

  • 1 Passport size & 2 ID size photographs (blue background)
  • Attested copy of SSC marks sheet
  • Attested copy of character/provisional certificate
  • CNIC copy of father (and applicant for BS programs)

General Instructions

  • Applying does not guarantee admission.
  • False/incomplete forms will be rejected even after fee payment.
  • No migration allowed from Rawalpindi/Islamabad colleges.
  • Admission is not allowed on a casual basis.
  • Separate forms must be submitted for multiple program choices.
